Discover our journey of faith, our mission to serve, and our commitment to spreading God's love in our community and beyond.

Our History

Our Story & Vision

Founded in 1985, Divine Sacred Ministries began as a small gathering of believers in a local community center. Through God's grace and the dedication of our members, we have grown into a vibrant community of faith serving thousands of families across our region.

Our vision is to create a sanctuary where all people can encounter God, grow in their faith, and serve their community with compassion and love. We believe in the transformative power of the Gospel and its ability to change lives, heal relationships, and build a better world.

At Divine Sacred Ministries, we are committed to biblical teaching, heartfelt worship, authentic community, and compassionate outreach. We welcome people from all walks of life to join us on this journey of faith.

Our History

Celebrating over three decades of God's faithfulness and ministry impact.

1985

Ministry Beginnings

Divine Sacred Ministries was founded by Pastor John Smith and a small group of believers meeting in a community center.

1992

First Church Building

After years of growth, we purchased and renovated our first dedicated church building to accommodate our expanding congregation.

2001

Community Outreach Expansion

Launched our comprehensive community outreach program, including a food pantry, counseling services, and youth mentorship.

2010

New Sanctuary Construction

Broke ground on our current sanctuary to accommodate our growing congregation and expanding ministry programs.

2018

Global Missions Initiative

Expanded our mission work internationally, establishing partnerships with churches and ministries in three countries.

2023

Digital Ministry Launch

Launched our online campus and digital ministry platforms to reach a global audience with the Gospel message.
