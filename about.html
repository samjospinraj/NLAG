Discover our rich history, mission, and the values that guide our community of faith, hope, and love.

Explore Our Story

A Legacy of Faith and Community

Founded in 1952, Royal Grace Cathedral has been a beacon of hope and spiritual guidance for generations. Our journey began with a small group of faithful believers who envisioned a sanctuary where all could experience God's grace and love.

Today, we continue that legacy through worship, community outreach, and spiritual development programs that transform lives and strengthen our community.

Our History

Celebrating over 70 years of faith, service, and community

1952

Foundation

Royal Grace Cathedral was founded by Rev. Thomas Anderson with just 35 members meeting in a small rented space.

1965

First Building

Our first permanent building was completed, providing a dedicated space for worship and community gatherings.

1980

Expansion

A major expansion added the fellowship hall and educational wing to accommodate our growing congregation.

2005

Cathedral Completion

The current cathedral building was completed, featuring stunning architecture and enhanced facilities.

2020

Digital Ministry

We launched our online ministry, reaching a global audience with live-streamed services and digital resources.
